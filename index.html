Specializing in foreign and domestic late model new and used parts.

Scott's Auto Inc. is a locally owned, family run business that has been serving the community since 1993. We pride ourselves on having one of the cleanest recycling facilities in the country, and offer industry leading parts and service.

  • 90-day warranty standard on most parts
  • 1-year, 3-year and lifetime extended warranties available
  • Next day local delivery from Colorado Springs to Laramie, WY
  • Same day shipping on most orders placed by 2 p.m. Mountain time
  • Nationwide freight shipping on large items
  • Cleaned, tested, quality late model used parts from the mid-2000's up to today's current makes of foreign and domestic vehicles
