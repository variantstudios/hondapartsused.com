Scott's Auto Inc. has a full service and repair facility. We have been repairing Hondas and Acuras since 1993 and have opened our doors to all makes and models since 2007. We also have an extensive used parts inventory available to help our customers offset the cost of repairs. Please feel free to call us for price quotes and scheduling.

Since we use OEM parts we can service Lease cars and New cars while still retaining your factory warranty.
